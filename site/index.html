Sell digital value, not shelf stock

Megasoft Market is a reseller portal — not a consumer shopping site. Authenticated partners browse telco and digital product catalogs, run promotion bundles, and credit airtime or prepaid value directly to a subscriber's mobile number or account.

What it does

Promotion bundles

Dashboard of live promos — title, operator, country, date range, and linked product bundles. Resellers drill into terms and eligible SKUs.

Product catalog

Digital products with wholesale pricing, benefits, validity, availability zones, and operator metadata — sourced from an upstream value-services API.

Instant credit

Purchase flow credits the end user by mobile_number or account_number. Transactions post to an async transactions API with auto-confirm.

Who is it for?

B2B resellers and distributors — telecom agents, prepaid aggregators, or fintech partners who need a branded UI on top of a digital value supply API. Admins get transaction history and balance views; standard users buy and credit only.
