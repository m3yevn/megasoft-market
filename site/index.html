Megasoft Market is a parody reseller portal — AI prompts, 3D models, meme NFTs, and other absurd SKUs. Full mock catalog, Vue 3 shop at /app, optional PayNow/TNG consensual gifts (not purchases).

What it does

Parody catalog

12 mock products across AI prompts, 3D models, freebies, and meme NFTs — same B2B shape as the original telco portal, totally different vibes.

Mock acquire flow

“Credit to email” transaction API returns instant fake delivery. No upstream DVS API. Suggested donation amounts are hints, not prices.

Voluntary gifts

PayNow or Touch ’n Go QR via env vars. Pitched as consensual donations — not a store, not financial advice, not a business.

Who is it for?

Portfolio demo enjoyers — and anyone who appreciates a B2B telco portal cosplaying as an AI/3D nonsense marketplace. Login with demo / demo.
